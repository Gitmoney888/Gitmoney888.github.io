Our History

Since 2021 we have served the best pizza in the midwest! We started out of a garage and have quickly grown to over 24 stores. Our mission is to provide affordable and delicious pizza for the entire world!

Our Prices

  • Personal - $3
  • Medium - $5
  • Large - $7
  • Xtra Large - $8

These are base prices for a thin or hand tossed one topping pizza. Deep dish is an additional $1.50 charge and additional toppings are $1.25 a piece.
